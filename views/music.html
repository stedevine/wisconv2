Six new songs recorded during the smog storm last summer, you should listen to this thing in one go in the dark.

Part II of the trilogy of singles we recorded with Brandon Eggleston. There is a monster in your closet.
My First Day (2017)
Anti-fascist synth punk. How we'd run things were it down to us. Recorded with Conrad Uno (Presidents, Mudhoney, etc.)
Wake Up (2016)
The first of our trilogy of hit singles. Recorded with Brandon Eggleston (Mountain Goats). Stacked harmonies and fuzzy synths.
Double Brother/Pigeon Control (2015)
Double A side single. Available on obsolete vinyl. Come to a show and pick one up.
Disc (2015)
Our first album. Grimy, lo-fi. Recorded over several months in Alex Upton's (former Wiscon drummer, mench) basement. All killer. Full of hidden gems that have dropped off our set list.
Defcon One (2012)
The first thing we ever recorded. Gets a little play on KEXP. Features left-handed bass by Aaron Morgan.
