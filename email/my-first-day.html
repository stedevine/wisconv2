After witnessing America's humiliating slide toward fascism Wiscon asked the question "What would we do if we were in charge?"
The answer came roaring back as their latest single, the proceeds of which will be donated to Southern Poverty Law Center - the best way the band feel they can fight hate groups.
It was recorded on Dec 30th by the legendary Conrad Uno (Presidents, Murder City Devils, Mudhoney...) at Egg Studios in Seattle and mastered by Jamie King. It has the band's usual focused, joy-wave sound but this time the sweet vocals carry a message:
"I'd end the war on women and reassure our children that they are safe in their own land"

Bio

Wiscon is the best band for the beach at and end of the world party. Their bomp sounds like a siren from 60s AM radio fronting a rawking house band revving up the score for a screening of the best sci-fi and horror B-movies. Mash up The Myserians, The Screamers, and The Ronnettes and you got this street level power pop soul fire
