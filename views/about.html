Wiscon was what happened when an unstoppable riff met an undeniable hook

Wiscon was the best band for the beach at and end of the world party. Their bomp sounded like a siren from 60s AM radio fronting a rawking house band revving up the score for a screening of the best sci-fi and horror B-movies. Mash up The Myserians, The Screamers, and The Ronnettes and you had this street level power pop soul fire!
