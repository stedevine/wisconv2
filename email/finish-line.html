Wiscon - final show and EP release

Wiscon

Goodbye forever!

Last show and EP release on May 11th at LoFi


Happier times at KEXP - Photo by Melissa Wax, courtesy of KEXP.

Hello, we're done. We love you. It's over

After creating genre-defining songs and playing bar after bar across the 206 for almost ten years we've decided that it's time to stop.

Yeah. I know.

But we're a class act and we're going out with some ceremony.

First we've put together an unmissable final show. Check out this line up:
Gibraltar massive indie heroes
Trash Fire young pretenders and your new favorite band
Clutch Douglas! reformed for one night only (because we still have some pull)
We also have Base Tan on the ones and twos. So it's going to be that kind of night

Not enough?

In a move that will surely break us in to the big time - we're releasing our last, best EP "The Finish Line" produced by Steve Fisk(!) at this show - come by and pick up an ultra-rare first edition physical copy before people catch on and it's reissued on 180gram vinyl.

And still you demand more?

It's prom-themed, so dress sharp.

Massive thanks to everyone who's supported us, we wouldn't have made it this far without you.

Bio

Wiscon was the best band for the beach at and end of the world party. Their bomp sounded like a siren from 60s AM radio fronting a rawking house band revving up the score for a screening of the best sci-fi and horror B-movies. Mash up The Myserians, The Screamers, and The Ronnettes and you had this street level power pop soul fire
